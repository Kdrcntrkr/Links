Bit Notepad — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Bit Notepad is a simple notes app. Your notes are stored only on your device using Apple’s on‑device storage (SwiftData). We do not collect, transmit, or share your data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Note titles and note content you create in the app.

All notes stay on your device unless you manually back up or transfer your device.

Analytics

We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.

Third‑Party Services

We do not integrate with third‑party services.

Biometrics

If you enable biometric protection, authentication is handled by the device’s secure system APIs. We do not access or store biometric data.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we add features that require data collection, we will update this policy and indicate a new “Last updated” date.
